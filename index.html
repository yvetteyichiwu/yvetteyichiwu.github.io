I'm Yvette (she/they), a phD student in linguistics at Harvard University, and parent to a small black cat named Luna ‍.

My research interests include:

  • morphology and syntax: agreement and case, adverbs, voice systems
  • language variation, contact, and change: style and indexicality, speaker attitudes
  • history of Austronesian languages: morphosyntactic alignment, topicalization

I work with the Tgdaya Seediq community in Taiwan, focusing on the use of nature and art in revitalization.
